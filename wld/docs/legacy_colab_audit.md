## Why the legacy metrics are exploratory

1. RNA is an encoder input and is added directly to the predicted final state.
2. HVG selection, RNA pseudotime, and optimal-transport pairing precede the
   train/test split.
3. The binary AUPRC labels use thresholds calculated from held-out targets.
4. The claimed ATAC-to-gene gate is generated by a random projection rather
   than genomic peak-to-gene links.
5. CollecTRI regulatory interactions are described as motif/binding evidence.
6. The one-token attention layer cannot attend across cells, genes, or TFs.
7. The neural dynamics branch can bypass the circuit mask.
8. The degree-matched permuted circuit performs better than the true circuit
   on all three saved metrics in the final control.

The final control is therefore a useful negative result: this architecture and
evaluation do not demonstrate dependence on the curated circuit prior.

## Corrected scope

The corrected PBMC runner evaluates ATAC-only RNA state reconstruction. It
performs training-only feature selection, deterministic regulatory-prior
construction, physical proximity peak-to-gene mapping, degree-preserving
TF-gene permutation, held-out ATAC shuffling, and paired multi-seed reporting.
It reports temporal, AUPRC, fixed-point, and attractor metrics as not applicable.

Temporal circuit dynamics remain a separate model contract that requires real
longitudinal, lineage-traced, metabolic-labeling, or perturbation-resolved data.
